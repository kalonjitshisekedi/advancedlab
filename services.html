Sample analysis & assaying

We provide high-precision chemical analysis for geological and metallurgical samples. Using advanced spectroscopy and wet chemistry techniques, we determine elemental composition with extreme accuracy.

  • Fire Assay for Gold and PGM
  • X-Ray Fluorescence (XRF)
  • ICP-OES and ICP-MS Analysis
  • Carbon and Sulphur Analysis

Process mineralogy

Understanding mineral deportment is crucial for recovery. Our mineralogy services characterise ores to predict processing behaviour and identify causes of metal loss.

  • Automated Mineralogy (SEM-EDS)
  • Optical Microscopy
  • X-Ray Diffraction (XRD)
  • Grain Size Distribution
